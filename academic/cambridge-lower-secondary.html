About the Programme

Building Skills for the Next Chapter

Cambridge Lower Secondary develops skills in 11 subjects — including English, Mathematics and Science — preparing learners for the next step of their education with a broad and balanced curriculum that builds on the foundations of Cambridge Primary.

At JPIC, the Lower Secondary years are a time of growing independence and deeper subject knowledge. Our teachers bring the curriculum to life with flexible, contextualised teaching that builds on each learner's strengths while preparing them for the rigour of Cambridge Upper Secondary.

Assessment

Tracking Progress Towards Upper Secondary

Cambridge Lower Secondary offers four assessment types, helping teachers monitor learner performance and report progress with confidence throughout Stages 7, 8 and 9.

01 · Measure Potential
MidYIS is an on-screen baseline assessment for ages 11–14 that identifies learning needs, measures potential and helps predict future performance in Cambridge IGCSE, O Level and International AS & A Level.
02 · Provide Feedback
Formative assessment guidance throughout the year helps teachers give meaningful feedback on skill development so students can reflect on and improve their performance.
03 · Monitor Progress
Cambridge Lower Secondary Progression Tests assess knowledge and understanding in English, Mathematics and Science across Stages 7, 8 and 9, at any time of year.
04 · Check Achievement
Cambridge Lower Secondary Checkpoint monitors performance at the end of the programme. Results are benchmarked internationally, and learners receive a statement of achievement and a diagnostic feedback report.
