About the College

A Legacy of Excellence
in Cambridge Education

St. John Paul II International College stands as one of Sri Lanka's leading providers of Cambridge International Education. Situated in Wennappuwa, we offer an environment where intellectual curiosity is celebrated and every student is empowered to reach their highest potential.

"Discover quality education at St. John Paul II International College — offering holistic learning experiences grounded in Catholic values and academic excellence."

Our college is built on a foundation of Catholic values, academic rigour, and a commitment to holistic development. With highly qualified faculty, world-class Cambridge curricula, and a nurturing community, we prepare students for universities and careers across the globe.

How to Apply

Your Admissions Journey

01
Enquire & Explore

Contact our admissions office or submit an online enquiry. We'll share our prospectus, programme details, and answer any initial questions.

02
Campus Visit & Interview

Schedule a campus tour to meet our faculty and see our facilities. Students may be invited for a short academic assessment or informal interview.

03
Submit Application

Complete and submit the application form along with required academic records, a passport photograph, and original report cards.

04
Offer & Enrolment

Receive your offer letter, complete registration formalities, and prepare to join the St. John Paul II family for the upcoming academic year.

Documents Required
  • Certified copy of the Birth Certificate
  • Three passport size photographs
  • Copy of the Baptismal Certificate
  • Parish Priest's Letter / Letter from Buddhist Monk
  • Leaving Certificate
