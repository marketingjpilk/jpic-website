Life at JPIC

More Than Education —
A Formation of Character

At St. John Paul II International College, student life is rooted in our Catholic identity and guided by the values of St. John Paul II — truth, compassion, service, and excellence. Beyond the classroom, every student is nurtured to grow in faith, leadership, and community.

Student life at JPIC extends far beyond academics. Guided by our Chilaw Diocese mission and our Cambridge International ethos, we nurture the whole person — intellectually, spiritually, socially, and morally. Our programmes build confident, compassionate young leaders ready to make a positive impact in the world.
