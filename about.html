OUR STORY

A Decade of Cambridge Excellence

St. John Paul II International College, Wennappuwa, was founded on 10th September 2015 by Very Rev. Fr. Neville Coonghe. The school was established with a clear and purposeful vision — to deliver the Cambridge curriculum, a globally recognised standard of education trusted by universities and institutions worldwide.

The College opened its doors with four dedicated teachers and twenty-five students, offering classes from LKG through to Primary II. From these humble beginnings, the passion for internationally recognised education grew steadily — and so did our community.

In 2023, a landmark moment was reached when our first cohort of Cambridge Ordinary Level students sat their qualifications — a proud testament to the College's academic journey and the dedication of our students and staff.

In 2025, St. John Paul II International College proudly celebrated its 10th Anniversary — a decade of inspiring learners, building futures, and serving our community with faith, truth, wisdom and love.

OUR VISION

Creating a God-loving society based on human dignity.

We envision a world shaped by faith, compassion and integrity — where every graduate of JPIC carries with them a deep reverence for God, a respect for human dignity, and the knowledge and skills to serve their community and the world.

OUR MISSION

To gift a holistic education to society based on Christian values.

Our mission is to provide an education that nurtures the whole person — intellectually, spiritually, socially and emotionally — grounded in the teachings of Jesus Christ and delivered through the globally recognised Cambridge curriculum.

CORE VALUES

Guiding Principles for Excellence and Integrity

Four pillars that define who we are, how we learn, and how we serve.

Faith
Faith in God, Treasuring Holiness

Faith in God is our foundation, guiding us to treasure holiness and integrate these core values into our Catholic education, fostering spiritual growth and moral integrity.

Truth
Understanding the Reality

Embracing truth and understanding reality are essential in our Catholic education, empowering students with knowledge, critical thinking, and a commitment to authenticity and integrity in life.

Wisdom
Realization of the Integrity of Life

Realizing the importance of integrity in life, our Catholic education fosters wise decision-making, moral development, and ethical principles, shaping students into compassionate and responsible individuals.

Love
Love for God and Fellow Human Beings

Embracing love for God and fellow human beings, our Catholic education nurtures compassion, empathy, and a strong sense of community, fostering harmonious and meaningful relationships.

BISHOP'S MESSAGE

A Message from Our Bishop

❝

Our schools are more than just institutions of learning; they are sanctuaries of faith, knowledge, and community.

Dear Students, Faculty, Staff, Parents, and Guardians,

As the Bishop of our cherished Catholic Diocese, it is with great joy and gratitude that I extend my warmest greetings to each member of our school community. Our schools are more than just institutions of learning; they are sanctuaries of faith, knowledge, and community. Rooted in the Gospel teachings and the rich traditions of our Catholic faith, our schools serve as beacons of light, guiding our students on their journey of spiritual and intellectual growth.

I am immensely proud of the dedication and commitment displayed by our educators, specially Rev. Fathers of our diocese whose unwavering passion for teaching not only imparts knowledge but also nurtures the hearts and souls of our students. Their selfless efforts in fostering an environment of love, respect, and compassion reflect the essence of our Catholic identity.

To our students, you are the future leaders of our Church and society. As you walk the halls of our schools, may you be inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ to embrace empathy, humility, and service to others. Your potential is boundless, and I encourage you to pursue excellence in all aspects of your academic, spiritual, and personal endeavors.

Parents and guardians, your partnership with our schools is invaluable. Your unwavering support and involvement in your child's education are integral to their success. Together, let us continue to uphold the values of faith, integrity, and community that define our Catholic identity.

As we navigate the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead, let us remain steadfast in our commitment to fostering a culture of inclusivity, diversity, and acceptance within our school community. By embracing our differences and celebrating our shared humanity, we can truly embody the message of love and unity that Jesus taught us.

In closing, let us entrust our schools, students, faculty, and staff to the loving embrace of our Heavenly Father, who guides us with His wisdom and grace. May His blessings continue to abound in our lives, and may we always strive to be beacons of light in a world that yearns for hope and compassion.

With prayers for abundant blessings,
Rt. Rev. Dr. Don Wimal Siri Jayasuriya
Bishop of the Diocese of Chilaw, Sri Lanka

PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

A Message from Our Principal

❝

Our vision is to cultivate a culture of excellence in education and integral formation where students are inspired to reach their fullest potential and become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

Dear Parents, Students and Staff,

As the Principal of St. John Paul II International College, it is my distinct pleasure to welcome you to our school community. At our school, we are committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where every student can thrive academically, spiritually, socially, and emotionally.

Our vision is to cultivate a culture of excellence in education and integral formation where students are inspired to reach their fullest potential and become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens. We believe in fostering a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom and empowers students to make meaningful contributions to the world around them.

At the heart of our educational philosophy is the Cambridge curriculum, renowned for its rigorous academic standards and emphasis on critical thinking, creativity, and innovation. Through our partnership with Cambridge Assessment International Education, we are proud to offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for success in an ever-evolving global landscape.

As we have embarked on this journey together, I am confident that our dedicated faculty and staff will provide the guidance, support, and inspiration needed to help each student achieve their goals. Our commitment to academic excellence is matched only by our dedication to fostering a safe, inclusive, and supportive learning environment where every student feels valued and respected.

I encourage you to explore our website to learn more about our programmes, extracurricular activities, and the rich array of opportunities available to our students. Whether you are a prospective parent, student, or member of our school community, we invite you to join us in our pursuit of excellence in education.

Thank you for choosing St. John Paul II International College as your partner in education. Together, let us continue to inspire, empower, and educate the next generation of leaders and innovators.

Best regards,
Rev. Fr. Isanka Mihiran Fernando
B. Ph (Rome), B. Th (Rome), STL (Rome)
Principal, St. John Paul II International College
