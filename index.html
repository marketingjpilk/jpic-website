About JPIC

A school built on
Truth, Wisdom and Love

Founded in 2015 under the Diocese of Chilaw, St. John Paul II International College is a Catholic institution offering Cambridge International Education in the heart of Wennappuwa, Sri Lanka.

We believe every student deserves an education that nurtures their academic potential, spiritual growth, and moral character. Our partnership with Cambridge Assessment International Education ensures our students are prepared for universities and opportunities worldwide.

Why Cambridge

Six reasons Cambridge is the right choice

01

Qualifications Recognised Worldwide

Cambridge qualifications are recognised and valued by the world's top-ranked universities and higher education institutions — opening doors to a world of opportunities for every learner.

02

Setting a Global Standard

With over 160 years of experience delivering fair, valid and reliable assessments to students worldwide, Cambridge offers a globally recognised performance standard for international education.

03

Your Path, Your Way

Schools can adapt the Cambridge curriculum, high-quality resources and flexible assessment to their local context — helping every learner reach their potential and thrive.

04

Learning With Lasting Impact

Cambridge learners build deep subject knowledge and conceptual understanding, developing a broad range of skills, learning habits and attributes that prepare them for life and work.

05

Improving Learning Outcomes

Trusted baseline and diagnostic assessments — together with data insights and evaluation tools — help teachers turn data into knowledge and actionable decisions that improve learner outcomes.

06

A Community of Experts

Cambridge brings together the collective knowledge of educators across 160 countries — supporting them to learn from one another, share ideas and continuously improve teaching and learning.
